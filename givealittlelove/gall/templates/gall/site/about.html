Happiness, Laughter, and Love Ambassador Program. Striving to empower individuals to make the world a more thoughtful and caring place.

It starts with a thought -- how can we bring our Mission, Vision, and Values outside of our walls and into our everyday lives? How can we positively impact the world with more than our products and websites? How can we help make this rude, and thoughtless world a more thoughtful and caring place?

The Happiness, Laughter, and Love Ambassador Program is designed to help us achieve this mission. The Program is simple, and it starts with you. You receive a card and you enroll. Once you're enrolled all you have to do is look around and find someone who could use a little Happiness, Laughter, and Love. Say hello. Pay them a compliment. Buy them a coffee. It doesn't matter. Just do something thoughtful and meaningful. Once you've done that, give them your card. Your card is their gateway into the program, and their opportunity to pay it forward.

The idea of paying it forward is very central to the program. To keep the movement going, every person will receive a small token of appreciation once they've payed it forward -- a free card from Cardstore.com. While Ambassador's aren't eligible for this reward, you are able to see all of the connections made from your initial thoughtful act. A little reminder of how a small action can affect great change.

We hope you're as excited about this opportunity as we are, and are ready to start spreading Happiness, Laughter, and Love today!
